About Me

Haris Haider

My name is Haris Haider and I am a 2021 graduate from the University of Glasgow. Completed my masters in MSc Financial Technology (Fintech). I belong from a relatively young and developing country Pakistan when compared to different economies in the world, but I believe that with time and the right kind of experience and exposure; I can become one of the few individuals that can help direct Pakistan to a more Financially developed and innovative nation by acting as a bridge with the rest of the world. That being said prior coming to the University of Glasgow I had completed my Bachelors in Majors in Finance and Minor in Mathematics & Statistics with my passion for supply chain management , strategical management , Finance and innovation . I chose to do my masters in a Degree in Financial Technology due to its upcoming trend and impact on the financial world. With buzz words floating around in my undergraduate like Artificial intelligence, big data, and Machine learning I had to explore my curiosity as well as what the world had to offer. After completing several of my modules such as big data analytics, Advances in Machine Learning, Artificial intelligence, Business Models for Innovation in Finance and Digital Entrepreneurship I have concluded to that I might lack the technical aspects of branches in Machine learning and Artificial but with the right incubation environment would love to explore such avenues . Additionally I enjoy the strategical formulation and implementation of innovative and trending technologies that can change and innovate the culture of business and Finance. The effects of how innovation can change the world drastically is something I want to be a part of. I personally would love to work at such a firm as this that helps promote innovation and support its people for future growth on a professional as well as personal level I feel my experience at university, professional career and life experiences allows me to be a suitable candidate to enter the organisation with my sets of skills, business knowledge and flexibility.
